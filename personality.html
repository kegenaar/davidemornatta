I am a big fan of the 16 personalities framework. I find it a useful lens for self-reflection.
The types should not be seen as rigid labels, but a starting point for understanding how you think, decide, and relate to others.
I also love using this framework to understand how other people behave in order to be able to better relate to them and make them feel seen.

The eight questions below are a rough approximation. Answer with what feels most natural, not what you think you should be. Results are only shown to you, nothing is stored.
